Introduction

I am Yuxuan ZHANG (张雨轩), a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence at the National University of Singapore and a research intern at AMD Singapore. My research focuses on robotics, computer vision, and human-centered AI, with an interest in embodied intelligence and deployable intelligent systems.

Before joining NUS, I received my Bachelor's degree in Intelligent Test & Control Engineering from the Harbin Institute of Technology, where I developed a foundation in embedded systems, distributed systems, measurement and control, and automation.

I am currently seeking Ph.D. opportunities in robotics and AI, and I welcome discussions on research collaboration, doctoral supervision, and related opportunities.
